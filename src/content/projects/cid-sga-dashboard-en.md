### Overview
Developed during a professional internship at CID, this dashboard was engineered as a core component of the "System Gestion des Affaire". The project provides the General Secretariat (SGA) with a centralized command center to transform raw administrative data into clear, actionable visual insights.

### Role-Based Analytical Insights
The system is built on a sophisticated role-based architecture designed to provide targeted intelligence to different stakeholders. I engineered specialized dashboards where each role such as the **Pole Manager** is presented with the specific analytics and performance indicators essential to their objectives. This ensures that leadership can monitor high-level organizational health while managers maintain a granular focus on the metrics most relevant to their specific tasks and projects.

### Technical Implementation
The platform utilizes a robust **Spring Boot** backend to process and aggregate data from a **PostgreSQL** database. On the frontend, I used **Angular** to build a responsive, professional-grade interface that handles complex data sets with high performance. The focus was on ensuring data integrity while providing a seamless, intuitive experience for executive stakeholders.

### Key Features
* **Customized Role Dashboards:** Individualized analytical views tailored to the specific needs of Project Managers and executive leadership.
* **Real-Time Reporting:** Automated data aggregation that replaces manual spreadsheets with live operational metrics.
* **Executive Visibility:** Engineered to simplify technical data for non-technical leadership, improving overall strategic alignment.
* **Enterprise Security:** Built on a secure Full-Stack architecture to protect sensitive institutional records.
