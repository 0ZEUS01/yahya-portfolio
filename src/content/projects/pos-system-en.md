### Overview
Developed and successfully commercialized as a freelance project, this Point of Sale (POS) system was engineered to modernize the operations of local retail shops. The platform provides a professional grade alternative to manual bookkeeping, offering merchants a reliable, high-performance environment to manage sales and stock.

### Commercial Inventory Logic
The core of the system is a robust database architecture designed for high-speed retail transactions. I engineered a real-time inventory tracking module that automatically updates stock levels with every sale, preventing discrepancies. To further streamline the checkout process, I integrated barcode scanning functionality, allowing for rapid product identification and minimizing human error during peak business hours.

### Technical Implementation
Built using **C#** and the **.NET** framework, the application is designed for stability and long-term reliability on Windows-based hardware. I utilized **SQL Server** to ensure data integrity for years of transaction history and stock records. The project represents a full lifecycle delivery from initial client requirements to successful commercial sales and local deployment.

### Key Features
* **Commercialized Solution:** A proven product sold to and utilized by multiple local retail businesses.
* **Automated Checkout:** Integrated barcode scanning for fast, error-free product entry and customer service.
* **Real-Time Stock Management:** Sophisticated SQL Server architecture for live inventory monitoring and reporting.
* **Professional Desktop UI:** A responsive C# interface optimized for high-frequency daily use in a retail setting.
