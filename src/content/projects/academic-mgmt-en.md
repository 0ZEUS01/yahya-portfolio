### Overview
Developed as a specialized freelance mission, this platform is a comprehensive management ecosystem for tutoring institutions. The system was designed to digitalize every administrative touchpoint, moving the school from fragmented tracking to a unified, role-based digital command center.

### Role-Based Command Centers
The architecture centers on a granular permission system where every staff member interacts with a custom-tailored dashboard. I engineered specific modules for the finance team to monitor student tuition, track teacher salaries, and manage overall school expenses. Simultaneously, academic administrators have dedicated tools to oversee classroom logistics, ensuring that each department has exactly the data and controls they need to operate efficiently.

### Technical Implementation
The system leverages the security and flexibility of the **Django** framework to handle complex user permissions and school logic. By integrating **SQL Server**, I ensured that sensitive financial records and personal student data are stored with enterprise-grade integrity. The full-stack implementation provides a seamless experience for managers to oversee all school operations from a single, secure web interface.

### Key Features
* **Departmental Dashboards:** Customized interfaces for finance, teaching, and administrative roles to manage their specific tasks.
* **Financial Oversight:** Specialized tools for the finance team to track tuition payments, payroll, and school-wide budget health.
* **Teacher & Schedule Management:** Automated assignment tools to link instructors with students and available classrooms.
* **Freelance Engineering:** A custom-built solution delivered to meet specific business requirements and operational workflows.
