Created a modern Android application for recipe enthusiasts. Built with Material3 design principles, Kozinti allows users to discover, create, and manage cooking instructions and ingredients. The app leverages Firebase for real-time cloud storage and authentication, combined with local storage access for high-quality recipe imagery.
