### Overview
Developed at the Faculty of Sciences and Techniques Errachidia (FST), this platform was designed to modernize university administration. It serves a dual purpose: providing an intuitive guidance portal for students while simultaneously offering a robust backend system for faculty members to manage teaching assignments and administrative records.

### Dual-Portal Architecture
The platform is split into two distinct environments to serve the entire university community. The public facing website acts as a resource hub, guiding students through institutional programs and academic opportunities. Behind the scenes, I engineered a secure management system where faculty members can track teaching loads, manage schedules, and streamline academic reporting. This centralized approach replaced fragmented manual processes with a unified digital workflow.

### Technical Implementation
The system leverages the **Laravel** framework's robust MVC architecture and security features to handle diverse user roles. I designed a comprehensive **MySQL** database to maintain the complex relationships between students, faculty, and academic programs. Using **Bootstrap**, I ensured that both the student portal and the administrative dashboard are fully responsive and accessible across all devices.

### Key Features
* **Student Guidance Hub:** A public-facing portal designed to improve access to institutional resources and academic programs.
* **Faculty Load Management:** Specialized administrative tools for tracking and managing teaching assignments and workloads.
* **Role-Based Security:** Secure access controls built with Laravel to separate public student data from private faculty records.
* **Responsive Design:** A mobile-friendly interface developed with Bootstrap for seamless use by students and staff alike.
