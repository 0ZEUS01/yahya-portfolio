### Overview
Developed during a specialized internship at Ark-x Talent Solutions, this project involved building a full-lifecycle insurance management system. The solution manages everything from initial lead generation to policy issuance and claims processing.

### Technical Implementation
The system leverages **Apex triggers** and **Lightning Web Components (LWC)** to provide a dynamic user interface. I implemented complex business logic using **Salesforce Flows** to automate the transition from a 'Lead' to an active 'Customer' and handled the automated routing of insurance claims for approval.

### AI Integration & 24/7 Support
To elevate the customer experience and streamline support operations, I integrated **Salesforce Agentforce**. This AI-driven implementation acts as the first point of contact, providing 24/7 automated assistance for policyholders. The agent can instantly address routine customer inquiries, guide users through the initial steps of the claim submission process, and intelligently escalate complex issues to human agents, significantly reducing overall response times.

### Key Features
* **Full Lifecycle Management:** End-to-end processing across Sales and Service Clouds.
* **Intelligent 24/7 Support:** Agentforce integration for round-the-clock claim assistance and automated customer service.
* **Automated Claims:** Flow-based automation for claim creation, validation, and approval/rejection.
* **Multi-Cloud Integration:** Seamless data flow between Sales, Service, Experience, and Data Clouds.
* **Custom Experience Site:** Built a portal for customers to manage their policies and file claims directly.
